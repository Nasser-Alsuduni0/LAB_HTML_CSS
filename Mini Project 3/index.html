Introduction

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming industries, society, and everyday life. From smart assistants to self-driving cars, the AI revolution is here.

Impact on Society

  • Automation of jobs
  • Improved healthcare diagnostics
  • Enhanced customer experiences

The Future of AI

The future promises advanced AI systems capable of reasoning, learning, and collaborating with humans in creative ways.

Did You Know?

AI has been around for decades. While AI is often thought of as a new technology, the concept has been around since the 1950s. Early AI systems were used to perform tasks such as playing chess and solving mathematical problems.
